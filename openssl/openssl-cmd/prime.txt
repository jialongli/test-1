32.36 prime
检查一个数是否为素数。示例如下：
openssl prime 79
openssl prime -hex 4F

			[root@localhost openssl-cmd]# openssl prime 79
			4F is prime
			[root@localhost openssl-cmd]# openssl prime -hex 4F
			4F is prime
			[root@localhost openssl-cmd]#
